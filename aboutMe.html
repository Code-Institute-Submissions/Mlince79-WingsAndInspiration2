About me

I was born in the early '80s in Chiapas, Mexico. I'm a daughter of a Reiki-healer and my father an agricultural pilot. Sister of two younger siblings and aunt of a little dancing and shining girl. I'm a mother of an amazing and magical boy. Wife of my Swedish best friend and loving life partner. I have a bachelor's degree in Marketing and a Master's in International Business Management. Currently a finance specialist and life coach for expat women.This is me, my roots, and connections on earth.

The reason I am an expat coach is because I am an expat too. I have gone through the process of understanding a new culture, language, getting a job, coping and then back to re-defining myself without losing my essence.
